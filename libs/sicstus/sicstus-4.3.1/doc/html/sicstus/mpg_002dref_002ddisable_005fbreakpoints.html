11.3.64 disable_breakpoints/1   [development]

Synopsis

disable_breakpoints(+BIDs)

Disables the breakpoints specified by BIDs.

Arguments

BIDs
list of integer, must be ground

Breakpoint identifiers.

Exceptions

instantiation_error
type_error
in BIDs
