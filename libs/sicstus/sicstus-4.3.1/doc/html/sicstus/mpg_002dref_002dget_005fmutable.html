11.3.96 get_mutable/2

Synopsis

get_mutable(-Datum, +Mutable)

Datum is the current representation of the mutable term Mutable.

Arguments

Datum
term, must be nonvar
Mutable
mutable, must be nonvar

Exceptions

instantiation_error
Mutable is uninstantiated.
type_error
Mutable is not a mutable.

ref-lte-mut.
