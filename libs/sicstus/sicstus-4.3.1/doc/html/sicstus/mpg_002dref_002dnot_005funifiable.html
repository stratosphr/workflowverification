11.3.138 \=/2   [ISO]

Synopsis

+Term1 \= +Term2

Term1 and Term2 do not unify.

Arguments

Term1
term
Term2
term

Description

The same as \+ X = Y; i.e. X and Y are not unifiable.

Exceptions

None.
