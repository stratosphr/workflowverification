11.3.73 error_exception/1   [development,hook]

Synopsis

:- multifile user:error_exception/1.

user:error_exception(+Exception)

Tells the debugger to enter trace mode on exceptions matching Exception.

Arguments

Exception
term

Exceptions

None.
