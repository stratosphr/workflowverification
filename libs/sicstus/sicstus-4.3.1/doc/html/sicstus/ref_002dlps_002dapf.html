4.9.3 Associating Predicates with Files

Information about loaded files and the predicates and clauses in them is returned by source_file/[1,2]. source_file/1 can be used to identify an absolute filename as loaded, or to backtrack through all loaded files. To find out the correlation between loaded files and predicates, call source_file/2. See mpg-ref-source_file.
