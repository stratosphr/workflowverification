11.1.6 Other Fields

The Backtracking field, if included, describes how the predicate behaves on backtracking. If this field is omitted, the predicate is determinate (succeeds at most once).

The See Also field contains cross references to related predicates and/or manual sections.

Reference pages may also include Comments, Examples, and Tips fields, when appropriate.
